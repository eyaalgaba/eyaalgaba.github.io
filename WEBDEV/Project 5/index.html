What is Shoujo Dreams?

Shoujo Dreams is the go-to site for romance anime fans! Made for girls and women (but open to everyone), it shares updates on the latest and upcoming romance anime. Whether you're a longtime fan or new to the genre, Shoujo Dreams keeps you in the loop with all the dreamy stories you love.
